The Story

SBURBSim, like any good simulation, needs source information and examples to be accurate. The observatory, located topside on the Land of Cults and Chandeliers, exists to collect and catalogue all that data, in conjunction with the transmission array on the Land of Obsidian and Shadows.

PL and RS use the array to look for interesting sessions out in the Furthest Ring, making sure that the Sim project can be as true-to-life as possible.

Without it, the players of session 13 may never be able to find a way out of their failed game...
